FDM vs SLA

Printer Types & Materials

FDM (Filament)

  • Materials: PLA, PETG, ABS, TPU
  • Pros: Affordable, strong, easy to use
  • Cons: Visible layer lines, less detail
  • Best for: Prototypes, jigs, enclosures

SLA (Resin)

  • Materials: Standard, Tough, Flexible, Dental, Castable
  • Pros: Ultra-fine detail, smooth surfaces
  • Cons: Post-cure + handling/safety, cost
  • Best for: Jewellery, dental, miniatures
